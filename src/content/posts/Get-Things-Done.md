Getting Things Done (GTD) is a time management method, described in the book of the same title by productivity consultant [David Allen](https://twitter.com/gtdguy).

原理：大脑善于处理信息，提出想法，但是不善于存储信息，即我们常说的“好记性不如烂笔头”。
